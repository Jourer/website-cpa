About

Nice to meet you! I’m Olga Rusenchyk, CPA, CA.

Throughout my career of over 13 years in public accounting, I have had the privilege of working with businesses of various sizes, gaining valuable insights into their unique challenges and opportunities. This experience inspired me to create a firm, Rusenchyk CPA Professional Corporation, focused on helping businesses that don’t need a full-time accounting department but still require expert financial support.

My goal is to provide accurate, timely, and personalized services, allowing me to combine my passion for accounting and tax with a commitment to helping businesses navigate their financial challenges, streamline processes, and achieve their objectives.

Whether you are a small business owner or entrepreneur seeking guidance on navigating changing tax regulations or support in optimizing your financial operations, I would be honored to help you focus on growing your business while we take care of the numbers!
